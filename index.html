About Me

I am currently a student at Epicodus in Portland, OR. I grew up in the San Francisco Bay Area and received my degree from UC Riverside. After working in Southern California for a few years I made my way back to the Bay Area and eventually to Portland. I have worked in varying industries and have had extensive customer service training and experiance. I started looking into programming because I found myself completely lost whenever my husband or brother had a conversation (they are both programmers) and I wanted to at least be able to have an inkling of what they were talking about. I started doing some of the CodeAcademy tutorials and found that I really enjoyed them and decided to pursue programming as a career, so here I am! When I'm not coding I enjoy reading (a lot), I especially like historical fiction. I also enjoy brewing beer, drinking beer, exploring new restaurants, hiking and crocheting.
